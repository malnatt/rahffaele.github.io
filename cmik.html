CMIK

2021

The concept of this project was born as an attempt to present the city of Milan from an unusal perspective. Milan is stereotypically regarded as a grey city: we tried to dismantle this prejudice by creating a tourist guide that follows the criteria of color, making known to the user the unifying thread that links apparently distant places.

The content we selected is divided in four palettes, named after traditional elements of the city.

Hovering on an image will show the specific color of the place in the form of a hexadecimal.

Every palette comes with a customised map of the city
