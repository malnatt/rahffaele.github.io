Interactive experience map

2022

A tool to design and keep track monthly of a customer experience performance, enabling clients and the consulting agency to quickly identify critical issues and formulate adequate solutions that are measurable in time, continuing to supply meaningful data to the client over time, and comparing performances.

All intellectual properties belong to THE BOSTON CONSULTING GROUP S.R.L.

A quantitative survey, designed by BCG along with the client in the early phases of the project, will map the service/product experience. The survey will be converted in a CSV file. The CSV file’s data will fuel the content of the digital map. The structure of the journey is designed as usual by a designer in the dedicated section of the tool.

The tool uses two different views that offer different functionalities.

The Overview

It coincides with the landing page for the client. It is an overview of their journey. Its purpose is to show the journey from a high point of view, visualising at first sight the overall customer experience, differentiated by behavioural clusters, and get an idea of their high and low points. Relevance is given to the business goals. In this view, the visualisation leans more on the narrative of the journey rather than the diagnostic purpose.

The Focused view

In this view the user can dive in and specifically look for what is or isn’t working properly along the journey, comparing and analysing the different moments that act along it. It is a detailed visualisation of every moment of the customer experience.

For its purpose, the focused view uses two different level of visualisation, the first one that shows the curves and the second one that hides them when the user scrolls down, focusing exclusively on the moments.

Overview functions

Track the overall experience performance1

Track business objectives performance in relation to the experience, by using the Business Objective filter2

Track performance of each behavioural cluster, individually or together using the Behavioural Cluster Filter3

Focused view functions

Understand the weight of each moments in relationship to the whole experience, within every step of the journey, using the data directly displayed on the considered moment/s4

Understand where the journey under perform and formulate solution by clustering, isolating and re-order the moments, thanks to a comprehensive filter panel
