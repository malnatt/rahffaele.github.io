Orto Botanico di Padova

2021

For the designing of the identity system of the garden we decided to enhance the secular tradition of the place, for centuries used for the cultivation of medical herbs.

This enhancement was based in the first place on the choice of the colour palette and the logo which stylistically is a reference to the world of herbal medicine.

Wayfinding system

Different signs were designed based on the historical importance of the plant.At the base of each sign some icons help the visitor to orient through the itineraries.

The wayfinding system is designed to give information to the visitor in an organized, not-crowded and not-invasive way.
